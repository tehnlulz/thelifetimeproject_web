Common Questions

How is this different from therapy or coaching?

We're not therapy – we're a development platform that synthesizes the world's best wisdom and personalizes it for you. Think of it as having a mentor who's read every great book and knows exactly what you need to hear each day. Many users combine our app with therapy for even better results.

What makes the AI truly personalized?

Marcus AI doesn't just use your assessment results. It learns from your daily check-ins, tracks your progress, remembers what you've shared, and continuously adapts. Every piece of content is generated fresh – no two users will ever receive the same audio.

How much time does it take?

Just 10-30 minutes daily (you choose). Most men listen during their commute or morning routine. The AI coach is there whenever you need it, and weekly goal planning takes about 15 minutes on Sundays.

Is this based on real psychology?

Absolutely. We integrate evidence-based approaches including attachment theory, CBT, positive psychology, and neuroscience. We also include time-tested wisdom from philosophy and proven business frameworks. Everything is synthesized and personalized by AI trained on thousands of hours of content.

What if I'm skeptical about AI?

We get it. That's why we offer a 7-day free trial. The AI isn't trying to replace human connection – it's synthesizing human wisdom in a way that would be impossible for any one person to do. Think of it as a highly intelligent curator, not a replacement for real relationships.
